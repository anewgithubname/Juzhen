Juzhen TinyStories — a small model that tells stories

Two ~30M-parameter Transformers trained from scratch with Juzhen on the TinyStories corpus (BPE tokens): an autoregressive LM that writes left to right, and a masked discrete diffusion LM that starts from an all-masked window and progressively unmasks it. Start a story and watch each write the rest.

try: a curious cat · a tall tower · a little boat
The story appears here.
Character-simple, coherent English by design — TinyStories models learn grammar, characters and simple plots, not facts. Autoregressive: causal attention, top-k sampling, streamed token by token. Diffusion: bidirectional attention, MaskGIT-style progressive decoding (□ = still masked), streamed round by round. Backend: Juzhen (C++/CUDA), BPE-4096 tokenizer, HTTP.
