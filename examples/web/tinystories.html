Juzhen TinyStories — a small model that tells stories

A ~30M-parameter Transformer trained from scratch with Juzhen on the TinyStories corpus (BPE tokens). Start a story and it writes the rest.

try: a curious cat · a tall tower · a little boat
The story appears here.
Character-simple, coherent English by design — TinyStories models learn grammar, characters and simple plots, not facts. Backend: Juzhen (C++/CUDA), BPE-4096 tokenizer, streamed over HTTP.
